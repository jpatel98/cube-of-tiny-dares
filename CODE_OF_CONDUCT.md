# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Cube of Tiny Dares to be a small, welcoming project for people who like playful software and useful weird objects.

Participants are expected to be kind, constructive, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming to beginners.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep issues and PRs focused.
- Respect different skill levels and hardware setups.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or repeated off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block contributors who violate this code.

To report a problem, email: hello@axiontechnologies.ca
